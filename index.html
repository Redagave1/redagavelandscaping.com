At Red Agave, we don't just landscape; we engineer luxury outdoor living environments. As Austin's premier design-build studio, we craft bespoke exterior spaces across Westlake, Barton Creek, Tarrytown, Circle C, and the Texas Hill Country. From resort-scale pool surrounds and architectural hardscaping to custom fire features and cedar pergolas, our work is defined by precision. By managing every phase entirely in-house—from the initial 3D conceptualization to the final installation—we eliminate the friction of multiple contractors. We deliver a seamless experience and an uncompromising level of craftsmanship. That is the Red Agave difference.
