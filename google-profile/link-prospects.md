## Notes

1. **Domain Rating (DR) estimates** are approximate, based on typical ranges for each site category. For exact DR values, run the URLs through Ahrefs, Moz, or Semrush.

2. **LLM citation priority:** The platforms LLMs most frequently cite for Austin landscaping queries are, in order: (1) LawnStarter, (2) Yelp, (3) Expertise.com, (4) Angi/HomeAdvisor, (5) Anderson Landscape (direct competitor with strong content). Getting Red Agave onto platforms 1-4 is the fastest path to LLM visibility.

3. **Timing:** Listicle outreach is most effective in January-February (before spring season) and September-October (before year-end list updates). Guest post pitches are evergreen but tie to seasonal hooks when possible.

4. **Track results:** Use UTM parameters on all backlinks where possible. Monitor LLM citation changes monthly by querying ChatGPT and Perplexity with the AI prompts listed in keywords.md.
