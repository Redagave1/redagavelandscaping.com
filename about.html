Who We Are

Red Agave Landscaping is a full-service design-build landscaping company based in Austin, Texas. Founded in 2009, we specialize in creating luxury outdoor living environments for homeowners across the Austin metro area, including Westlake Hills, Barton Creek, Tarrytown, Circle C, Cedar Park, Round Rock, Bee Cave, and the Texas Hill Country.

We are not a lawn care company. We are designers and builders. Every project starts with a custom CAD design, and we handle every phase of construction in-house — from the initial consultation and 2D plan through hardscape installation, planting, lighting, and final walkthrough. There are no subcontractors. The same stonemasons, carpenters, horticulturists, and lighting technicians who start your project are the ones who finish it.

The Design-Build Difference

Most landscaping companies are either designers who hand off to a builder, or builders who work from your sketch. Red Agave is both. We call it design-build, and it means three things for you:

  • One team, one point of contact. Your project manager is your designer and your builder. No miscommunication between a design firm and a separate install crew
  • Free 2D CAD plan with every estimate. You see exactly what you are getting before any work starts. We revise until the design fits your property and your vision
  • No subcontractors. Every trade — stone, carpentry, electrical, planting, irrigation, steel fabrication — is handled by our full-time Austin-based crew

This approach eliminates the cost markups, scheduling conflicts, and quality inconsistencies that come with subcontracting. It is why we can offer a 24-hour response guarantee and why our projects finish on time and on budget.
