Our Services

Full-spectrum outdoor design and build services — xeriscape, decks, lighting, fire and water, outdoor living, and custom fabrication.

Austin's Most Complete Landscaping Service

Everything You Need

From the initial design consultation through the final walkthrough, Red Agave handles every aspect of your outdoor transformation. Each service below represents years of refinement, specialized expertise, and a genuine passion for pushing what's possible in outdoor spaces.

01
Xeriscape / Hardscape

Xeriscape is an art form that transforms your landscape into a sustainable, stunning outdoor space. We work with native plants, decomposed granite, limestone boulders, and welded steel accents to create designs that are both water-wise and visually dramatic. Our hardscape work includes patios, walkways, retaining walls, and custom features that define spaces and add lasting value to your property.

Xeriscape Design & Install Decomposed Granite Pathways Limestone Boulders Custom Steel Accents Native Plant Selection Retaining Walls Flagstone Patios

State of the Art Lighting

Go far beyond simple low-voltage lighting with our specialty lighting systems. We work with FCOB (Flexible Chip on Board), COB, and WLED technology to create dramatic, programmable lighting environments that transform your outdoor spaces at night. From subtle uplighting of specimen plants to full color-changing systems controlled from your phone.

WLED Smart Systems FCOB & COB Strip Lighting Tree Uplighting Pathway Lighting Color-Changing Systems App-Controlled Scenes Deck & Step Lighting

Custom Built Decks
