The Challenge · Westlake Hills

8-foot grade. Zero masonry.

Bento-Box Geometry · Zero Visible Hardware

A luxury residential property in Westlake Hills required a modern, structural solution to overcome a severe 8-foot grade decline across heavy Central Texas clay and limestone sub-layers. The homeowner sought a seamless extension of their minimalist indoor living space.

The brief: clean bento-box geometry, zero visible lighting hardware, and zero off-the-shelf masonry components. Every element custom-fabricated. Every finish architectural. The entire build executed 100% in-house — zero subcontracted trades.

Where most contractors would propose a CMU block wall with a stone veneer and call it done, Red Agave engineered a tiered structural steel system that reclaimed 1,200 square feet of usable outdoor living space from a slope that was previously unusable.

Engineering Solution

Structural steel. Not masonry.

Structural · 01 Retaining System 1/4" Corten · Deadman Anchors 1/4-inch heavy-gauge Corten steel plates welded to subterranean I-beam posts set inside 36-inch deep reinforced concrete footers. Horizontal deadman tie-backs extend 6 feet into compacted aggregate. Hydrostatic relief via washed aggregate backfill and solid-core PVC collector pipe network.
Zero Subcontracted Trades
12 days. Turnkey.
System Details

Uncapped Steel Retaining

  • —1/4-inch Corten steel plates, field-welded to I-beam posts
  • —36-inch deep reinforced concrete footers
  • —6-foot deadman anchor tie-backs into compacted aggregate
  • —Washed aggregate backfill + PVC hydrostatic relief network

Marbled Concrete + Floating Steps

  • —4-inch poured-in-place marbled concrete slabs ($14/sq ft)
  • —Artisan marbled surface finish — high visual contrast against dark aggregate
  • —Cantilevered floating steps over lower gravel beds
  • —Concealed steel sub-frames beneath each step tread

24V FCOB Lighting

  • —IP67 waterproof 24V FCOB silicone tape in milled channels
  • —Under steel coping lips + cantilevered step treads
  • —ESP32 micro-controller running WLED firmware
  • —2700K warm-white ambient via app-based control

Specimen Botanical Curation

  • —Whale’s Tongue Agaves (Agave ovatifolia)
  • —Artichoke Agaves (Agave parryi)
  • —Mediterranean Fan Palms (Chamaerops humilis)
  • —1-inch Black Star basalt gravel ($390/yard) + 6-inch turf margins
