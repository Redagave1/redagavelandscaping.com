Design Intent · East Austin, TX

Pool, porch, kitchen. Every element connected.

East Austin · Full Outdoor Transformation

This East Austin backyard had potential that far exceeded its original form. As the pool came together, we designed the surrounding environment around it — screened porch, teak deck, outdoor kitchen, and a custom Corten steel screen — each piece planned as part of one connected outdoor room.

A glass-wall screened room anchors the back of the house, with tongue-and-groove cedar on the ceiling and a ceiling fan for year-round use. From inside, the pool is framed like a picture. The teak deck wraps the pool perimeter and connects both zones without a step.

On the far end, a custom Corten steel screen — perforated in a geometric dot pattern — is backlit with LED strip so it reads as art by day and a glowing panel after dark. The outdoor kitchen sits beneath a red sail shade: built-in grill, under-counter fridge, string lights tracing the cedar privacy fence.
