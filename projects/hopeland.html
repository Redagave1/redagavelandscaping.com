Hopeland

Residence · West Lake Hills 5,400 sq ft · 14 week build
Scroll
Design Intent

A pavilion carved from the grade.

Hopeland began as a question: how do you hold a room outside. The answer was to build one — walled in cedar, shaded by steel, grounded in stone — and then let the garden close in from every side.

Every material was chosen for how it behaves as dusk arrives. Warmth in the cedar, weight in the limestone, and a quiet glow from integrated lighting that keeps the pavilion occupied long after sundown.
