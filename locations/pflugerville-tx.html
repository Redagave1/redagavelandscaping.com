About Our Work in Pflugerville

Design + Build Landscaping
for Pflugerville Homeowners

Pflugerville has evolved from a quiet Austin suburb into one of the most dynamic communities in Central Texas. New developments like Blackhawk, Avalon, and Villages of Hidden Lake sit alongside established neighborhoods with mature lots, and the range of properties here means no two projects look alike. Red Agave brings the same luxury-grade craftsmanship to Pflugerville that we deliver across the Austin metro — from full backyard transformations to targeted upgrades like fire pits and landscape lighting.

Our Axehandle project in Pflugerville is one of our signature builds — a complete outdoor environment that showcases what's possible when design, materials, and execution all align. That project features custom hardscape, xeriscape plantings, integrated lighting, and outdoor living elements that turned an underused backyard into the best room on the property.

Pflugerville's diverse lot sizes and soil conditions — from black clay in the east to rockier ground near the western edges — mean every project requires site-specific planning. We handle grading, drainage, and irrigation as part of the design process, not as afterthoughts. The result is a landscape that performs as well as it looks, year after year.

What We Build

Our Services in Pflugerville

Xeriscape & Hardscape

Water-efficient native plantings, decomposed granite, flagstone, and custom steel edging. Designed for Pflugerville's variable soil conditions and summer heat.

Custom Decks

Composite, cedar, and IPE deck systems with integrated benches, privacy screens, and railing details. Built to handle Central Texas weather extremes.

Fire Pits & Fire Features

Custom gas and wood-burning fire pits, linear fire tables, and fire bowls. Stone, concrete, and steel builds that anchor the outdoor living space.

Pergolas & Outdoor Living

Freestanding and attached pergola structures with fan and lighting integration. Seating walls, planters, and shade systems that extend your living square footage.

Outdoor Kitchens

Built-in grills, refrigeration, bar seating, and prep areas with stone veneer and concrete countertops. Complete outdoor cooking and entertaining setups.

WLED Landscape Lighting

WLED and FCOB lighting systems for paths, trees, structures, and entertaining areas. Warm, layered lighting that extends your outdoor hours.

Full Yard Design + Build

Complete backyard transformations from blank slate to finished environment. Design, permitting, and construction managed as a single cohesive project.

Custom Planters

Corten steel, concrete, and cedar planters at any scale. Freestanding or integrated into hardscape for architectural plantings and privacy screens.
