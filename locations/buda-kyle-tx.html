About Our Work in Buda & Kyle

Quality Landscaping
for Buda & Kyle Properties

Buda and Kyle are two of the fastest-growing communities in the Austin metro — family-oriented cities south of the river where new construction is redefining the landscape along the I-35 corridor. Neighborhoods like Plum Creek, Waterleaf, Cypress Forest, and Sage Meadow are filling with homeowners who want outdoor spaces that stand out from the builder-grade norm. Red Agave brings the same design standards and craftsmanship we deliver in Westlake Hills and Lakeway to every project in Buda and Kyle, scaled to fit the property and the budget.

The terrain in south Hays County is transitional — the rocky limestone of the Hill Country gives way to the gentler Blackland Prairie, with heavier clay soils, flatter lots, and different drainage challenges than the hill communities to the west. We adapt our approach accordingly, selecting native and adapted plants that thrive in these soil conditions and designing drainage solutions that keep water moving away from foundations on the flatter grades typical of newer subdivisions.

New-construction homes in Buda and Kyle often come with minimal landscaping — bare yards, basic sod, and no outdoor living features. That's where we come in. From custom deck builds and pergolas to complete yard transformations with xeriscape, fire pits, and landscape lighting, we turn blank-slate properties into outdoor spaces that actually get used. Every project gets the same attention to detail, the same quality materials, and the same design thinking — regardless of the price point.

What We Build

Our Services in Buda & Kyle

Xeriscape & Hardscape

Drought-adapted native plantings, natural stone, decomposed granite pathways, and steel edging. Designed for Buda and Kyle's clay-heavy soils and new-construction lot conditions.

Custom Decks & Elevated Structures

Composite, cedar, and IPE deck systems sized for Buda and Kyle's family-oriented lots. Covered patios, elevated platforms, and multi-use outdoor spaces built to last.

Pergolas & Outdoor Living

Cedar and steel pergolas, shade structures, and outdoor rooms that extend the living space beyond builder-grade patios. Designed for the south Austin sun and year-round use.

Fire Pits & Water Features

Natural stone fire pits, gas fire tables, and water features that anchor the backyard. Custom builds that transform new-construction yards into genuine outdoor destinations.

Outdoor Kitchens

Outdoor kitchen builds with stone veneer, concrete countertops, built-in grills, and weatherproof storage. Designed for weekend cookouts and family entertaining in Buda and Kyle.

WLED Landscape Lighting

Low-voltage WLED systems that add security, ambiance, and curb appeal. Path lighting, uplighting, and accent lighting designed to elevate new-construction properties.

Pool Design & Construction

Custom pool builds for Buda and Kyle backyards. Plunge pools, family-sized pools, and complete pool-and-patio packages designed for the lots and budgets common in south Hays County.

Full Yard Design + Build

Complete yard transformations from blank-slate to finished. We coordinate grading, drainage, hardscape, planting, lighting, and irrigation — turning builder-grade lots into something worth coming home to.
