About Our Work in Round Rock

Design + Build Landscaping
for Round Rock Homeowners

Round Rock is one of Central Texas's fastest-growing suburbs, and its homeowners are investing in outdoor spaces that match the energy of the community. From established neighborhoods near Old Settlers Park to the newer master-planned developments along University Boulevard and La Frontera, Red Agave brings the same design-forward craftsmanship to Round Rock that we're known for across the Austin metro.

Round Rock lots tend to run larger than those inside Austin's urban core, which means more room for ambitious designs: expansive xeriscape installations with native grasses and decomposed granite, full outdoor kitchen builds, multi-level deck systems, and fire features that anchor the backyard as a true living space. The limestone-heavy soil common throughout Williamson County presents unique grading and drainage challenges that our crews handle daily.

Whether you're working within HOA guidelines in Teravista or Forest Creek, or you have the freedom to go bold on a larger lot in the 79681 corridor, we design landscapes that thrive in Round Rock's climate and complement the architecture of the home. View our Signature Projects to see the level of detail we bring to every build.

What We Build

Our Services in Round Rock

Xeriscape & Hardscape

Water-wise native plantings, decomposed granite, custom steel edging, and stone pathways designed for Round Rock's limestone soil and hot summers.

Custom Decks

Composite, cedar, and IPE deck systems built to handle Round Rock's heat and UV exposure. Multi-level designs with integrated seating and privacy walls.

Pergolas & Shade Structures

Freestanding and attached pergolas that extend your living space. Stained cedar and steel hybrid builds with optional lighting integration.

Fire Pits & Water Features

Custom gas and wood-burning fire pits, linear fire tables, bubbling boulders, and water walls that bring texture and sound to the backyard.

Outdoor Kitchens

Built-in grills, bar tops, refrigeration, and full outdoor kitchen setups with stone veneer and concrete countertops.

WLED Landscape Lighting

State-of-the-art WLED and FCOB lighting systems. Path lighting, uplighting, moonlighting, and architectural accent lighting.

Full Yard Design + Build

Complete backyard transformations from concept to completion. We handle design, permitting, and construction as a single team.

Pool Design & Construction

Custom pool and spa builds integrated with the landscape design. Geometric and freeform shapes with water features and decking.
