# Monthly Citation & Brand Mention Check

Check for Red Agave's presence across key citation sources and directories.

## Steps:

1. **Search the web** for "Red Agave Landscape Austin" and "Red Agave Landscaping Austin TX" — note which directories and sites mention the business.

2. **Check key citation sources:**
   - Google Business Profile (search Google for the business name)
   - Yelp
   - BBB
   - Houzz
   - Angi (formerly Angie's List)
   - HomeAdvisor
   - Thumbtack
   - Nextdoor
   - Facebook
   - Instagram (@redagaveatx)
   - YouTube (@redagavelawnandlandscape3227)

3. **For each source found**, note:
   - Is NAP (Name, Address, Phone) consistent? Phone should be 512-695-7566.
   - Is the website URL correct (redagaveatx.com)?
   - Are there reviews? How many and what rating?
   - Is the business category correct (landscaping/landscape design)?

4. **Search for brand mentions** — look for "Red Agave" + "Austin" + "landscape" in news, blogs, forums, social media. Note any unlinked brand mentions (opportunities to request a backlink).

5. **Check competitor citations** — for top 3-5 competitors found in search results, note which directories they're listed on that Red Agave isn't.

6. **Output a report:**
   - Citation consistency score
   - Missing citations (directories to submit to)
   - Unlinked brand mentions (backlink opportunities)
   - Review count summary across platforms
   - Action items prioritized by impact
